FIFA WORLD CUP 2022

The 2022 FIFA World Cup is scheduled to be the 22nd running of the FIFA World Cup competition, the quadrennial international men's football championship contested by the senior national teams of the member associations of FIFA. It is scheduled to take place in Qatar from 21 November to 18 December 2022. This will be the first World Cup ever to be held in the Arab world.

General informations:

  • Host country: QATAR
  • Dates: 21 November - 18 December
  • Teams: 32 (from 5 confederations)
  • Stadiums: 8 (in 5 host cities)
  • Official ball: ADIDAS AL RIHLA
